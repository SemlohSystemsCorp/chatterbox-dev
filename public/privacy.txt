1. INTRODUCTION

This Privacy Policy describes how Semloh Systems Corp (“Semloh,” “Company,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, discloses, and protects information in connection with Chatterbox (the “Service”), an enterprise communications platform.

This Privacy Policy applies to:

* Organizational customers (“Organizations”)
* Authorized users of Organizational accounts (“End Users”)
* Website visitors
* Prospective customers

If you use the Service through an Organization, your Organization controls your account and the data processed within it. In those cases, Semloh acts primarily as a service provider (or “data processor”) on behalf of the Organization.

2. ROLE OF THE ORGANIZATION

If you access Chatterbox through your employer or another Organization:

* The Organization is the data controller of Customer Data.
* The Organization determines what data is collected and how it is used.
* The Organization may access, modify, export, or delete your communications and data.

Questions about your Organization’s data practices should be directed to your Organization’s administrator.

We use cookies and similar technologies for:

* Authentication
